# ✨ Runes & Rummy

**Runes & Rummy** is a fantasy-inspired single-player Gin Rummy experience where players enter a magical duel against an AI opponent known as **The Dark Wizard**. Combining traditional rummy mechanics with immersive fantasy aesthetics, the game challenges players to strategically create melds and runs while minimizing deadwood through tactical draws, discards, knocking, and gin declarations.

The experience features dynamic light and dark fantasy environments, shimmering visual effects, glowing timers, responsive controls, and difficulty-based soundtrack systems that evolve throughout each duel. Players can customize gameplay settings including music, sound, haptics, difficulty level, hand size, and theme modes while tracking moves and elapsed duel time in real time.

## 🎮 How To Play

The objective of **Runes & Rummy** is to defeat **The Dark Wizard** by creating melds and runs while keeping your deadwood score as low as possible. During each turn, players draw a card from either the draw pile or discard pile, then discard one card from their hand to improve their combinations.

As the duel progresses, players must strategically decide which cards to keep, discard, or use to complete stronger hands. The game automatically tracks deadwood points, highlights valid melds and runs, and records move history throughout the match.

When a player reaches a low enough deadwood score, they can choose to **Knock** to end the round or declare **Gin** with a complete hand for the highest possible victory. The duel ends when either the Player or The Dark Wizard wins the round, followed by the option to begin a new duel.

## 🛠 Tech Stack

- **HTML5** — Provides the core structure and layout for the game interface and duel system.  
- **CSS3** — Handles fantasy-themed styling, responsive layouts, shimmer animations, glow effects, light/dark environments, and custom UI design.  
- **JavaScript** — Powers gameplay logic including turn systems, deadwood calculations, meld detection, scoring, timer functionality, move tracking, and AI behavior.  
- **LocalStorage API** — Stores gameplay preferences such as theme settings, sound options, difficulty selections, and player configurations.
- **Audio API** — Manages dynamic soundtrack playback, looping music systems, sound effects, and interactive audio controls.   
- **AI Game Logic** — Controls The Dark Wizard’s decision-making, card selection, and duel interactions.  
- **Vercel** — Handles deployment, hosting, and automatic production updates.
